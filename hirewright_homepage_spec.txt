# HIREWRIGHT — HOMEPAGE COPY & STRUCTURE SPEC

This document contains the full homepage copy, section structure, and design notes for the Hirewright employer-facing website (main domain). The candidate-facing site lives on a separate subdomain and is not covered here.

---

## BRAND BASICS

**Company name:** Hirewright
**Tagline (short, for footer):** Skilled industrial work, delivered properly.
**Established:** 2001
**Primary market:** German industrial companies
**Audience:** HR managers, plant managers, operations directors, procurement
**Languages:** English (EN) + German (DE) , make the buttons for both options, but only focus on English for now
**Tone:** Restrained, factual, German B2B register. No marketing fluff. Numbers do the work. Short declarative sentences.

---

## COLOR PALETTE

- **Navy** `#0A2540` — primary, ~70% of usage
- **Near-Black** `#0D0D0D` — body text, anchor
- **Off-White** `#FAFAFA` — background
- **Signal Amber** `#F59E0B` — functional accent only (CTAs, highlights, link hovers), keep under 5% of any layout

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

- **Display / Headlines:** DIN Next Bold (preferred) or Oswald Bold (free fallback)
- **Body / UI:** Inter (Regular 400, Medium 500, Semibold 600)
- All caps reserved for industrial signage moments (wordmark, labels, small section headers)
- Letter-spacing: tight on display, normal on body, wide (3-4) on small-caps labels

## RESPONSIVE BREAKPOINTS

- **Desktop:** 1280px+ — full layouts as described

- **Mobile:** <768px — single column, sticky header reduced height, CTAs stack vertically

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES (for the developer/designer)

1. **Generous whitespace.** German B2B sites breathe. Don't compress sections.
2. **Typography over imagery.** Hero and section headers do the heavy lifting. Photography is a supporting role, not the main act.
3. **No stock photos of handshakes, glass-walled meeting rooms, or smiling office workers.** If photography is used, it should be real industrial environments — production lines, shipyards, workshops. Black and white or muted tones, not high-saturation marketing photography.
4. **Icons must be consistent.** Single line-style icon set, single weight, navy color. No mixing filled and outlined.
5. **No animations on scroll unless they serve content** (e.g., the map can have subtle line animation; numbers should NOT have counter animations).
6. **Amber is sacred.** Use only on CTAs, prices, key numbers, and the right-angle accent in the monogram. Overuse breaks the brand.

## TECH STACK SUGGESTIONS (optional)

- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router) or Astro for static performance
